About me

I'm a graphic design graduate from DeMontfort University with a passion for creativity and aesthetics. Currently, I'm venturing into the world of coding with The Coders Guild, expanding my skill set and combining design with technology. But my learning won't end there; I am always seeking new challenges and opportunities to grow.
As a designer-turned-coder I have the advantage of understanding both the visual aspects and the technical aspects of design. This fusion allows me to craft user experiences that are not only visually stunning but also function seamlessly. Whether it's developing a responsive website or designing captivating digital assets, I strive to bring a harmonious balance of aesthetics and functionality to all my projects.
